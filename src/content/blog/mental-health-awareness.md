Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet many people hesitate to seek help. At Alstonville Clinic, we're committed to providing compassionate, confidential mental health support.

## Common Mental Health Concerns

We help patients manage a variety of mental health conditions, including:

- Depression and anxiety
- Stress and burnout
- Sleep disorders
- Grief and loss
- Adjustment disorders

## How We Can Help

Our approach to mental health care includes:

1. **Comprehensive Assessment**: We take time to understand your unique situation
2. **Mental Health Care Plans**: Access to Medicare-funded psychology sessions
3. **Medication Management**: When appropriate, in conjunction with therapy
4. **Ongoing Support**: Regular follow-ups to monitor your progress
5. **Referrals**: Connections to psychologists, psychiatrists, and support services

## Taking the First Step

The hardest part is often reaching out for help. Remember:

- Mental health challenges are common and treatable
- Seeking help is a sign of strength
- Your conversations with us are confidential
- Recovery is possible with the right support

## Make an Appointment

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, please reach out. We're here to help, without judgment.

**In an emergency, call 000 or Lifeline 13 11 14**
